# Write Category — Extra Axis: Tone

### tone (1-5)
Is the writing style appropriate for the intended context and audience?

- **1 — Inappropriate**: Completely wrong register (e.g., casual slang in a legal document, or stiff formality in a friendly email).
- **2 — Off-key**: Generally wrong tone with occasional acceptable passages.
- **3 — Adequate**: Tone is passable but inconsistent or slightly mismatched.
- **4 — Appropriate**: Tone matches the intended audience and context throughout.
- **5 — Perfect pitch**: Tone is precisely calibrated; reads as if written by a domain expert for the exact audience.

When scoring tone, consider:
- Does the formality level match the context (email, report, blog, legal, etc.)?
- Is the vocabulary appropriate for the target audience?
- Is the tone consistent throughout, or does it shift unexpectedly?
- Does the writing achieve the intended effect (persuade, inform, entertain)?
